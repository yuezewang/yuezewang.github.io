Biography

I am working on Large Multi-modal Model in Beijing Academy of Artificial Intelligence (BAAI). I obtained the B.S. degree in Communication Engineering from Tianjin University in 2020, and the M.S. degree in Information and Communication Engineering from Tianjin Key Laboratory of Brain Inspired Intelligence Technology, Tianjin University in 2023, under the supervision of Prof. Yanwei Pang.


My interests include Data-centric AI, Multi-modal Understanding / Generation / Retrieval, Computer Vison and Image Processing.


My preference is to facilitate various tasks mainly through Data Research & Data Engineering. This includes but are not limited to: data collection, data synthesis, data filtering/cleaning, data labeling/tagging, data quality inspection/analysis, data processing/construction, data augumentation/optimization, data selection/mixture and usage in training & subjective/objective evaluation.
