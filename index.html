Biography

I am working on Large Multi-modal Models in BAAI. I obtained the B.S. degree in Communication Engineering from Tianjin University, 2020, and the M.S. degree in Information and Communication Engineering from Tianjin Key Laboratory of Brain Inspired Intelligence Technology, Tianjin University, 2023, under the supervision of Prof. Yanwei Pang.


My interests include Data-centric AI, Multi-modal Understanding/Generation/Retrieval, Computer Vison and Image Processing.


My preference is to facilitate various tasks mainly through effective and efficient Data Research & Data Engineering. They includes: data collection | data synthesis | data filtering/cleaning | data labeling/tagging | data quality inspection/analysis | data processing/construction | data augumentation/optimization | data selection/mixture and usage in training & subjective/objective evaluation.
